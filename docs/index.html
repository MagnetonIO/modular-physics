Mathematical Composition Framework

Formal Composition Structure

f₁(I, R): E ≥ (ℏc ln 2)/(2πkR) · I

f₂(E₁, T): E ≥ max(E₁, kT ln 2 · I)

f₃(E₂, A): E ≥ E₂ · min(1, I/(A/4ℓ²ₚ ln 2))

f₄(E₃, ρ): E ≥ E₃ · Θ(ρ < c³/Gℏ ln 2)

Domain(f₁)
I ∈ ℝ⁺, R ∈ [ℓₚ, ∞)
Domain(f₂)
T ∈ (0, Tₚ]
Domain(f₃)
A ∈ [4ℓ²ₚ, ∞)
Domain(f₄)
ρ ∈ [0, ρcrit]

The composition f₄ ∘ f₃ ∘ f₂ ∘ f₁ creates a monotonic, convex function space preserving physical constraints at each level.
